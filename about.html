The pine city mall is the second largest shopping centre located in Westville near Durban and one of the largest in South Africa. The mall has gone through many phases of extensions. Due to the strategic location, the Pine city mall has seen considerable foot count growth. The Pine city mall is an iconic family-focused mall that is conveniently located in the warm, welcoming coastal city of Durban. The diverse cultural buy in to The Pine city mall also makes it one of the more accessible and inviting shopping centres. The Pine cities distinctive domes have become familiar glittering beacons, punctuating the skyline from afar and welcoming shoppers from throughout the area. Influenced by European architectural styles, The Pavilion has distinguished itself as a recognisable landmark since its opening in October 1993. It is one of the most established first-generation shopping centres within the Province.

The mall was developed in the early 90s and opened in October 1993 by Murray and Roberts, (now Concor), to initially provide 75,000 m2 (810,000 sq ft) of shopping space. Subsequently, the centre has gone through six phases of expansion, the most recent of which was completed in 2014, bringing the floor space to just under 135,000 m2 (1,450,000 sq ft). An additional 35,000 m2 (380,000 sq ft) expansion is planned for 2018

The Pine city mall is owned by Pareto, a leading retail industry investor and loan stock company in South Africa that is fully committed to enhancing super regional malls such as the Pine city mall.
